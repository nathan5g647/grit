🚀 What is GRIT?

GRIT is your personal training tracker that rewards effort, balance, and discipline — not just volume. From endurance athletes to everyday grinders, GRIT helps you stay consistent and sharp.

🔥 Key Features

  • Personal Dashboard: Log every training session with duration, intensity, HR, RPE and more.
  • GRIT Points: Earn points by training consistently and with purpose.
  • Fatigue Penalty: Overtrain? Your score drops. We reward smart athletes, not just grinders.
  • Leaderboard: Compete with your friends. See who’s putting in the work, day after day.
  • Streak Tracking: Daily, weekly, or monthly — track and beat your personal records.
  • Strava Integration: Connect and sync your workouts in one click.

🌍 Why Athletes Choose GRIT

Whether you're a skier, runner, cyclist, or lifter — GRIT makes your progress visible. It pushes you to show up, but never forgets that recovery is part of the game.

Backed by a smart fatigue-adjusted scoring system, GRIT helps you find your rhythm and maintain it over time.
